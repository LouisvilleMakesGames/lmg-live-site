What do we do?

Louisvlle Makes Games is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization committed to fostering an inclusive, supportive environment for game developers of skill levels in the Louisville area. Through game jams, meetups, kids' classes, and creative events, LMG provides opportunities for collaboration and skill development, helping to reshape the gaming industry by breaking down stereotypes and making game development accessible to everyone.
